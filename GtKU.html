Benefits*

          

    Competitive pay, fixed shift patterns

    As well as great pay, Amazon offers fixed shift patterns.You will have four days on and three days off.

    Full training

    Amazon provides on site training for everyone. You will also benefit from ongoing support from your agencies.

    Welcoming culture

    We have many Amazonians from different countries and ethnic backgrounds and value a diverse workforce.

    Have fun!

    No matter how busy times are there are fun events, competitions, prizes, giveaways, foodie treats and more!

    Working environment

    Great facilities in our Fulfilment Centres - free internet points, secure lockers, comfortable canteens and facilities to store and heat your own food.

    Subsidised canteen

    We offer subsidised meals, cold snacks and vending machines are available as well as free tea and coffee.

    Car Parking & transportation

    Free parking at all Fulfilment Centres. Transport available in some locations.



You are only four steps away from your temporary assignment at Amazon

Click the ‘View open jobs’ button to get the whole package!

1.Register and apply:
Click "View open jobs" and register directly on our application portal your preferred staffing agency and location.
2.Send application:
Check the information you've provided is correct. Sign your application with 'eSignature' by entering your full name and clicking on the "send application" button. You are done!
3.Receive invitation:
Our staffing agencies will review your application and get in contact with you as soon as possible. You will then receive an invitation from them to a hiring event.
4.Attend Hiring Event:
You’ll meet a staffing agency recruiter and if you’re successful, they will place you into your new temporary assignment to Amazon!
